## How to Get Started on ChemiCloud: Step by Step

**1. Go to their site and pick your plan.** Start with developer if you're serious about performance. The basic plan works fine for small blogs, but you'll notice it gets slower once you hit moderate traffic.

**2. Enter your domain name.** You can use an existing domain you own elsewhere, or buy one from them. If you're bringing an existing domain, don't switch nameservers yet. Just add it as addon domain first.

**3. Choose your billing cycle.** They offer monthly, annual, biennial, and triennial. The longer you pay upfront, the lower your per-month cost. I did annual and saved about 30% versus monthly.

**4. Go through checkout.** This is where they could be pushy but aren't. You'll see optional stuff like SSL (you don't need to buy it, it's free with Let's Encrypt) and other features. Skip them.

**5. Create your account and wait for activation.** It takes about 10 minutes. You'll get an email with your login info and FTP credentials.

**6. Install WordPress or whatever you're using.** ChemiCloud has Softaculous in cPanel, which is the easy way. It's one-click WordPress installation. Takes 2 minutes.

**7. Point your domain.** If you're using an existing domain registered elsewhere, change the nameservers to ChemiCloud's. If you bought the domain with them, it's already done. This can take 24-48 hours to fully propagate.
