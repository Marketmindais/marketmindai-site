## Step-by-Step Walkthrough

Using Dievio is pretty simple. Here’s how I typically run a lead generation campaign with it:

1.  **Sign Up and Choose Your Plan:** First, you need an account. Go to their site and pick a plan that matches how many leads you need. I started with the Pro plan for $49/month. This gave me enough credits to really test it out. It's a quick process, just email and password.
2.  **Access the Lead Finder:** Once logged in, you'll see a simple dashboard. Click on the "Lead Finder" or "Search" option. There aren't many other places to go, so it’s hard to get lost.
3.  **Set Your Search Criteria:** This is where you tell Dievio what kind of leads you want.
    *   **Job Titles:** Enter specific titles like "Marketing Manager," "CEO," or "Head of Sales." You can add several.
    *   **Industries:** Pick from a dropdown list or type in keywords like "SaaS," "Real Estate," or "Manufacturing."
    *   **Company Size:** Use the sliders or input boxes to define employee ranges, for example, 50-200 employees.
    *   **Location:** Specify countries, states, or even cities.
    *   **Keywords:** Sometimes I'd add extra keywords related to their business, like "e-commerce" or "AI solutions."
4.  **Run the Search and Review Results:** Hit the "Search" button. Dievio will pull up a list of contacts matching your criteria. You'll see names, job titles, company names, and often a basic email address. Take a quick look to make sure the results are helpful. If not, change your filters and search again.
5.  **Verify Emails (Optional but Recommended):** Before downloading, I always run the emails through Dievio's built-in verifier. Select all the leads you want to check and click "Verify Emails." This uses up your verification credits but saves you from sending emails that bounce later. It marks emails as "valid," "risky," or "invalid."
6.  **Download Your Leads:** Once verified, click the "Download" button. You’ll get a CSV file. This file contains all the data: name, job title, company, website, email address, and the verification status.
7.  **Import to Your CRM or Email Tool:** Now you have your list. I usually open the CSV, clean it up a bit (remove any invalid emails), and then import it directly into my cold email software or CRM. This gets your list ready for your actual outreach campaign.

That's it. From login to a clean CSV in hand, it's a very simple process.
